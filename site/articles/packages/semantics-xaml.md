# SemanticDiff.Semantics.Xaml

`SemanticDiff.Semantics.Xaml` extracts semantic anchors from XAML files.

## Responsibilities

- XAML type, member, binding, resource, and named-element detection,
- file-level and symbol-level navigation anchors,
- relationships between XAML resources, bindings, and code-behind symbols,
- fallback behavior for files without full semantic service coverage.
